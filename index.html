How it works

From inbound call to resolved case in seconds.

A five-minute demo of this flow is your best proof of value. Here's every step.

📲 Step 01

Caller reaches Amazon Connect

Voice or chat inbound hits your Connect instance. ClarityCompanion activates immediately on the contact flow.

🧠 Step 02

AI triages intent

Claude-powered orchestration understands the caller's need, searches your knowledge base, and determines the resolution path.

✅ Step 03

Resolves or escalates

80%+ of requests are resolved autonomously. Complex cases are warm-transferred to a human agent with full context.

📋 Step 04

Case auto-created in Salesforce

Transcript, resolution, sentiment, and follow-up tasks are written directly to Salesforce Service Cloud — automatically.
